NPC Cognitive Architecture

How Traits, Objectives, Tasks, Actions, and Traces connect

Key Relationships

Objectives → Tasks

An Objective like "kill someone" decomposes into Tasks: "choose target", "go to their home at 1am", "kill target", "go home". Each Task has a time window and priority.

Tasks → Schedule

When Tasks change, the Schedule recalculates. Higher-priority Tasks win conflicts. A crime task at priority 40 overrides the sleep task at priority 30.

Tasks → Actions

When the Schedule says "now", the Task executes as one or more Actions. Actions are the atomic unit that changes world state and produces Traces.

Actions → Traces

Every Action declares what Traces it may produce. "Buy drinks" → Item(receipt). "Kill person" → Mark(blood), Sighting(neighbors). Traces exist in the world independently after creation.
