City Map Visual Style

How should the top-down city map look at default zoom?

A: Clean Blocks

Solid colored rectangles for each building type. Roads are plain gray. Minimal detail — like a zoning map. Buildings are just colored shapes matching their type (brown houses, gray apartments, blue offices, yellow diners). Green background for grass. Trees are simple circles in parks.

B: Detailed

More visual detail: houses get roof triangles and doors, apartments get window grids, roads get center lines and sidewalk edges. More visually rich but more rendering work. Could slow down at full city scale.
