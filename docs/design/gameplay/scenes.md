Overview of all the major scenes the player can interact with.

## City scene
has sidebar, has city map with viewport on wider map, shows address nodes, allows travel)

## Address scene
has sidebar, shows art of current address, allows player to enter the address, interact with people, interact with crime scenes, stakeout the address, etc. Also lots of address-specific options. For example, when we add Airport addresses, the player wil lsee options to Travel To Another City. Submenus - for example, "investigate" enters a whole series of submenus for ways to investigate that address, or "shop" menu if you're at a store.

## Interior Scene
not sure if we will build this or not. If we do, it would have a sidebar, and would show a floorplan layout of the current location

## Conversation scene
has sidebar, shows options for talking to a person

## Globe Scene
has sidebar, shown when player is selecting travel location from airport or when they want to select to view locations at another city even while they are in their current city, depending on the context of the view (airport or data viewer) player can activate physical travel to another city or can click on a city to jump to viewing it to look at the addresses that they know about there.

## HQ scene
has sidebar, when the player joins the CIA, they can go to HQ to talk to the crypto department, get equipment, etc. - might just be a variant Address view or Interior Scene

## Hacking minigame
no sidebar, player fully immersed in fake-computer interface

## Tailing minigame
no sidebar, unique car-like user interface, similar to city view

## Electronics minigame
no sidebar, player is immersed in fake wire-cutting or similar minigame
